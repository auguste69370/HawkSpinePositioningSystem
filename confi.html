What are your rights regarding your Personal Data?

You have the right at any time to exercise with HAWKCELL SAS the rights provided for by the current applicable regulations in terms of personal data, subject to meeting the conditions (which is linked to the legal basis of the processing):

  • Right of access: you can have communication of your personal data subject to processing based on your consent, the execution of a public service mission, a legal obligation, the execution of your contract or legitimate interest;
  • Right of rectification: you can update your personal data or have your processed personal data rectified, based on your consent, the execution of a public service mission, a legal obligation, the execution of your contract or legitimate interest;
  • Right of opposition: you can express your wish that your personal data no longer be subject to processing in the case where the processing is based on your consent (you withdraw your consent) or on contractual execution (contract waiver clause) as well as in the case of processing carried out in the legitimate interest. However, you cannot oppose processing carried out within the framework of a legal obligation or within the framework of the execution of a public service mission which presents compelling and legitimate reasons prevailing over your rights and freedoms;
  • Right to erasure: you can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to the legal retention period, in the case where the processing is based on your consent (you withdraw your consent) or on contractual execution (contract waiver clause) as well as in the case of processing carried out in the legitimate interest. However, you cannot request the erasure of data from processing carried out within the framework of a legal obligation or the performance of a public service mission;
  • Right to limitation: you can request the suspension of processing of your personal data based on your consent, legal obligation, contractual execution or legitimate interest if you have a request for rectification, erasure or opposition in progress or if you consider the processing unlawful;
  • Right to portability: you can request to recover your personal data in order to dispose of it only if the processing is based on your consent or the execution of a contract. You cannot benefit from the right to portability in the case where the processing is carried out within the framework of a legal obligation, the execution of a public service mission or legitimate interest.

When subscribing to a service, ordering a Product or collecting your Personal Data, you are given the address (postal and/or electronic) to which to send your request to exercise your rights.

Any request must be accompanied by proof of identity. HAWKCELL SAS undertakes to respond to your requests to exercise your rights as soon as possible and in any case in compliance with legal deadlines.

Cookie Policy

HAWKCELL SAS websites and all their web pages use cookies to distinguish you from other website users. This allows us to improve our services and optimize your user experience.

A cookie consists of a string of information in text format transferred by a website to your computer's hard drive so that the website remembers you. Cookies can help a website adapt content to your interests more quickly. Most major websites use cookies. Generally, a cookie contains the original domain name of the cookie, the "lifetime" of the cookie and a value, namely a unique number created randomly.

On our websites, we use different types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: essential for the technical operation of our websites (e.g.: session identification, secure access, shopping cart, etc.). They do not require prior consent.
  • Audience measurement cookies (analytics): used for statistical purposes to measure the attendance of our websites, understand user journeys and improve our content. These cookies are only deposited if you have consented to them.
  • Personalization cookies: allow the display or content of our Web Platform to be adapted to your preferences (language, display choices...).
  • Advertising cookies: used to present you with relevant advertisements based on your browsing. These cookies require your prior consent.

You have the possibility to accept or refuse cookies that are not necessary for the operation of our services during your first visit to our websites, then this will be offered to you regularly.

Your choice is kept for a maximum period of 6 months, at the end of which you will be asked again. You can also modify your preferences at any time by clicking on the "Manage my cookies" link available at the bottom of the page.

You can also configure your browser to refuse all cookies or only certain types. Each browser offers specific modalities:

  • Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
  • Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/fr/kb/activer-desactiver-cookies
  • Safari: https://support.apple.com/fr-fr/HT201265
  • Edge: https://support.microsoft.com/fr-fr/help/4027947/microsoft-edge-delete-cookies

Please note that blocking certain cookies may degrade the user experience and limit certain functionalities of our websites.
