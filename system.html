The technical excellence
behind HawkSpine

Materials and construction

HawkSpine is manufactured from premium materials selected for their exceptional properties in medical environments.

Primary materials:

  • High-grade POM-C (Polyoxymethylene)
  • Medical-grade PlexiglaSS (PMMA)
  • Non-magnetic components

Medical imaging compatibility

Designed for seamless integration with medical imaging systems without interference.

Compatibility:

  • 100% CT scanner compatible
  • 100% closed-tunnel MRI compatible
  • No artifacts on images

Adjustable components

Positioning arms and supports designed with precision for optimal patient positioning.

Component features:

  • Dual support system
  • Height adjustment
  • Secure locking mechanisms
  • Hammock for small animals (< 10 kg / < 22 lb)

Cleaning and maintenance:

Designed for easy cleaning and sterilization in clinical environments.

Maintenance features:

  • Quick disassembly
  • Smooth surfaces
  • Washable parts
  • Chemical resistance*

*Non-degrading with medical-grade disinfectants

HawkSpine System
Requirements

Ensure your facility meets these requirements to guarantee optimal performance and safety

MRI and CT Systems

  • Tunnel diameter: ≥ 60 cm (23.6″)
  • Magnetic field: ≥ 0.5 T
  • Flat or curved MRI and CT tables
  • Compatible with most CT systems

Environment

  • Temperature: 15–25 °C (59–77 °F)
  • Humidity: 30–60 %
  • Clean Surfaces
  • Can be stored in the MRI or CT room

Training

  • MRI and CT safety basics
  • Easy patient positioning
  • Coil configuration usage
  • Anesthesia configuration for MRI/CT imaging

Important note

HawkSpine is designed exclusively for veterinary use. Please ensure system requirements are met before purchase. Technical team available for compatibility consultations.
